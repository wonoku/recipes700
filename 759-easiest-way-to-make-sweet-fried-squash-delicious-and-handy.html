Sweet fried squash step by step

  1. First deep fry the diced squash.
  2. Now in different pan roast cumin seeds+ginger+garlic+coriander seeds.
  3. After roast properly 2 to 3 minutes add green chilli and diced tomatoes.add sugar too.
  4. Add fried squash too and continue at low flame.cover the utensil.
  5. Check every after one minute and roast for 6 to 7 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves.
