Cookpad Anniversary Cake instructions

  1. Sift maida with cornflour,soda and baking powder. Keep aside.
  2. Beat milk maid. Gradually add sugar and bit till fluffy. Add essence.
  3. Add oil beat well..
  4. Add half Maida mix add half of the milk. Beat well..
  5. Add remaining Maida and milk beating well after the addition..
  6. Beat well 3-4 minutes till the mixture is smooth and light and thick pouring ribbon consistency.
  7. Add liquid soda mix quickly. Put mixture in the grease tin..
  8. Preheated microwave 180 degree and bake for 40 minutes. Check with toothpick..
  9. When cold the spong then Cut the cake sponge in two-three layers. Put sugar water on it..
  10. Take whipped cream beat it till the fluffy. Put whipped cream on each Layer..
  11. On the the top design with strawberry colour, gem, fruit. Yummy Cookpad Anniversary cake is ready to eat..
