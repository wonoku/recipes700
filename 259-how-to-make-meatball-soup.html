Ingredients of Meatball soup

  1. It's 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. It's 1 teaspoon of minced garlic.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of diced sweet onion.
  4. You need 1/4 teaspoon of ground pepper.
  5. You need 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  6. You need 1 can of petite diced tomatoes 15 ounces (not drained).
  7. It's 4 cups of beef broth.
  8. It's 3 cups of mixed vegetables or 3/4ths cup each: carrots, peas, green beans, corn.
  9. Prepare 1 package of meatballs about 25 bite size.
