Ingredients of Yoghurt Spaghetti *Vegetarian

  1. You need 500 gr of spaghetti.
  2. Prepare 1 kg of greek plain yoghurt.
  3. Prepare 250 gr of corn frozen kernels or 4 corns boiled, grained.
  4. Prepare 1 of garlic clove.
  5. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  6. It's of Oregano.

Yoghurt Spaghetti *Vegetarian step by step

  1. Boil spaghetti until done..
  2. Mash the garlic, mix it with spaghetti..
  3. Mix corn kernels (reheat the frozen corn kernels in microwave before mixing them with spaghetti), yoghurt, and salt with spaghetti, mix well..
  4. Put the spaghetti on the platter and sprinkle some oregano..
  5. Yoghurt spaghetti is ready to be served..
