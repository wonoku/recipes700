Ingredients of Oatmeal cookies

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of instant oats.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of gluten free all-purpose flour.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  4. You need 1 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  5. It's Pinch of salt.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of coconut oil.
  7. It's 1 of large egg (room temp).
  8. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of liquid stevia.
  10. Prepare to taste of raisins or chocolate chips.

Oatmeal cookies step by step

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Mix wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  3. Pour dry ingredients into the wet bowl, mix until well combined.
  4. Chill the dough in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven at 300 F.
  6. Distribute tablespoons of dough on the baking tray, leaving some space between them..
  7. Bake for 15 minutes, turn off the oven and leave tray inside for another 10 minutes.
  8. Cool down the cookies.
