Cucumber Salad #mommasrecipes step by step

  1. Slice the cucumber and onion.
  2. Put in a medium or large container with lid.
  3. Add whole grape tomatoes and as much dressing as desired.
  4. Put lid on and shake to combine.
  5. Chill and serve as an easy side.
