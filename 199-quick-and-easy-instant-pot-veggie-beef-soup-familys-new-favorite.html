Ingredients of Quick and easy instant pot veggie beef soup

  1. Prepare 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 of small yellow onion.
  3. You need 1 Tbsp of minced garlic.
  4. Prepare 1 (32 oz) of box of beef broth.
  5. You need 1 lb. of Frozen mixed vegetables.
  6. It's 1 (15 oz) of can diced tomatoes.
  7. Prepare 1 (4 oz) of can stems and pieces mushrooms.
  8. You need 2 of bay leaves.
  9. It's 1/2 tsp of dried thyme.
  10. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.

Quick and easy instant pot veggie beef soup instructions

  1. Turn instapot to saute mode. While its heating chop onion and mince garlic..
  2. When instapot is hot. Brown hamburger with onion and garlic..
  3. Add all other ingredients to pot and stir.
  4. Set instapot to manual setting with high pressure for 5 minutes..
  5. Let pressure release naturally for 5 minutes. Then release the rest of the pressure by turning it valve. (Caution the steam will be hot).
