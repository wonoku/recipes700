Ingredients of Crockpot garlic Parmesan chicken

  1. It's 3 of lbs. frozen chicken breasts.
  2. You need 1 cup of chicken broth.
  3. Prepare 2 tsp of Italian seasoning, divided use.
  4. Prepare 2 sticks (1 cup) of salted butter, softened.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of mayonnaise.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of minced garlic.
  8. You need to taste of salt and pepper,.

Crockpot garlic Parmesan chicken instructions

  1. Place frozen chicken breasts into the bottom of crockpot. Pour one cup of chicken broth over top..
  2. Season with 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning and a dash of salt and pepper..
  3. Cover and cook on high for 3 hours (or low for at least 6 hours)..
  4. Drain excess broth and fat from cooked chicken. In a bowl, combine softened butter, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, 1 tsp. Italian seasoning and a dash of salt and pepper. Stir to combine..
  5. Scoop this mixture on top of cooked chicken..
  6. Cover and cook on high for an additional 30 minutes until sauce is creamy and cheese is melted..
  7. Serve over egg noodles or rice..
  8. Notes- I used this. + the Italian seasoning..
  9. This can also be cooked in an instant pot or pressure cooker..
