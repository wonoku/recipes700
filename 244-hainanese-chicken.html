Ingredients of Hainanese Chicken

  1. You need 4 of chicken quarters.
  2. You need 2 knobs of ginger; sliced.
  3. You need 4-5 of garlic cloves.
  4. Prepare 2 bunches of green onions.
  5. You need of Salt.
  6. Prepare of White pepper.
  7. Prepare of Soy sauce.
  8. It's of Sesame oil.

Hainanese Chicken instructions

  1. Cut up 1 bunch of green onions into 3‚Äù pieces. Slice your ginger..
  2. Pat dry and season your chicken w salt on both sides. Sprinkle some white pepper if you fancy. Feel free to use different cuts of chicken too..
  3. Into the Instant Pot they go! Place your chicken on top of a trivet. Distribute your green onions and ginger between the chicken. Add 1 cup of water..
  4. Cook on high for 15mins..
  5. Meanwhile! Mince the other bunch of green onions and add to a heat-safe bowl. Grate in 1 knob of ginger + 1/2 a tsp of salt. Pour in 2 tbsp of hot oil to slightly cook the mixture. This is Sauce 1..
  6. Sauce 2: 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1/2 tbsp of sesame oil..
  7. Save your chicken juices in the pot!!!! Use it for soup or cook your rice in it. Srsly. Try it.
  8. Serve w a side of rice and sliced cucumbers. Enjoy hot or cold!.
