Ingredients of Vegetable Zucchini Soup

  1. You need Half of a Lg. Zucchini, cubed.
  2. Prepare 2-3 of Lg. Potatoes, cubed.
  3. You need 1 of Lg. Can Mixed Vegetables, drained.
  4. Prepare 1 of Lg. Can Whole Kernel Corn, drained.
  5. It's 1 of Lg. Can Chopped Spinach, drained.
  6. Prepare 2-3 Quarts of Chicken Broth.
  7. It's 6-7 of Beef Bouillon Cubes.
  8. Prepare 1 of Lg. Can Diced Tomatoes w/ Green Chiles.
  9. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of All Purpose Seasoning.
  10. It's 1 teaspoon of Onion Powder.
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon of Garlic Powder.
  12. You need 1 teaspoon of Dried Cilantro.
  13. It's 1 teaspoon of Lemon Pepper Seasoning.
  14. It's to taste of Salt and Pepper.

Vegetable Zucchini Soup instructions

  1. In a Large Stock pot add everything together and bring to a boil on high heat. Once to a boil lower heat to a simmer and let soup simmer covered til potatoes and zucchini is tender; stirring ever so often. Once vegetables are soft turn off heat and Serve with Crusty Buttered Bread..
