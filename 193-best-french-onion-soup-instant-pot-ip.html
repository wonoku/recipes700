Ingredients of French onion soup Instant Pot IP

  1. It's 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter.
  2. Prepare 2 of yellow onions chopped.
  3. Prepare 2 of red onions chopped.
  4. You need 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  5. You need 2 cups of white wine.
  6. Prepare 4 cups of beef broth.
  7. You need 4 of thyme sprigs.
  8. It's 2 of bay leaves.
  9. It's 1 TSP of kosher salt.
  10. It's 4 of garlic cloves minced (garlic lovers 8 cloves).
  11. Prepare 1 of baguette loaf.
  12. Prepare 4-6 slices of provolone cheese.
  13. It's of Garnishes.
  14. It's of Kosher Salt.
  15. Prepare of Fresh ground pepper.
  16. It's of Fresh parsley.

French onion soup Instant Pot IP instructions

  1. Melt butter in pressure cooker over medium saute setting..
  2. Add onions and baking soda until onions soften slightly and start to release liquid. Around 3 minutes..
  3. Seal pressure cooker and cook at high pressure for 20 minutes. No need to add liquid. The onions will release all the liquid needed. When finished quick release the pressure. Remove lid..
  4. Drain liquid then on high/more saute setting, continue to stir the onions till all the onions are sticky and brown..
  5. Add broth, white wine, salt, and garlic stirring and scraping bottom of the pot to remove any bits that may be stuck to bottom of the pot. Add thyme and bay leaves..
  6. Reseal instant pot and cook at high pressure for 10 minutes. When beeper sounds do a quick release of the pressure. Remove lid..
  7. Discard the bay leaves and thyme sprigs. Preheat oven to broil. Cut the baguette into 1 to 1 1/2 inch pieces...
  8. Ladle soup into oven safe bowls leaving room for the baguette on top. Add pieces of baguette (2 or 3) on top of soup. Cover with a slice of provolone cheese..
  9. Place bowl in oven and cook with oven door cracked until cheese is melted..
  10. Remove from oven. sprinkle salt, pepper, and parsley over the top..
  11. Enjoy!!.
