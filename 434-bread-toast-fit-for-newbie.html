Bread toast instructions

  1. Take a bowl mix all the ingredients.
  2. Pre heat the oven/ tawa.
  3. Take a slice of bread, spread mixed vegetables evenly on top of it.

  5. After 20 minutes take out the bread slices from oven/tawa, serve hot with tomato ketchup.
