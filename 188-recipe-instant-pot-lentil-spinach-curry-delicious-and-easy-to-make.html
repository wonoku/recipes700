Ingredients of Instant Pot Lentil Spinach Curry

  1. Prepare 3/4 cup of Moong dal (yellow lentil).
  2. Prepare 1 cup of Baby Spinach.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Olive oil.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Butter.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of Onion chopped.
  6. Prepare 1/2 of Tomato finely diced(optional).
  7. You need 1 tbsp of Minced ginger garlic.
  8. It's 2-3 cups of Water.
  9. It's 1 tsp of Turmeric.
  10. You need 1-2 tsp of Salt.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of Mustard Seeds.
  12. Prepare 1 tsp of Cumin seeds.
  13. Prepare 1 tsp of Garam Masala.
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Black pepper powder.
  15. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Asafoetida/Hing(Optional).
  16. You need of Chopped Cilantro.

Instant Pot Lentil Spinach Curry step by step

  1. Wash the lentils thoroughly and keep them drained..
  2. Keep the Instant Pot on Sauté mode.Once hot,add oil and butter.Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds and when they start spluttering,add the chopped onions and ginger garlic..
  3. When the onions become soft, add turmeric,salt,hing and black pepper along with 1/4 cup water and cook for another 2 minutes..
  4. When oil separates from the masala, add the lentils and 2 cups water into the inner pot..
  5. Close the IP lid and keep the steam release handle to Sealing position.Pressure cook for 4 minutes..
  6. Once done, open the lid using quick release.Stir the lentils and if the mixture is thick, add some water and let it simmer for a minute.Mash the lentils using a spoon to get a smooth consistency..
  7. Now add the spinach leaves and diced tomatoes and pressure cook for another 2 minutes and once done,open the lid using quick release..
  8. Add little water as required if the curry is too thick..
  9. Sprinkle some garam masala and chopped cilantro..
  10. Serve the curry hot along with some Rice..
