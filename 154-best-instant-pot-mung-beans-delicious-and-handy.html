  2. You need 5 cups of water.
  3. You need 1 of onion sliced.
  4. Prepare 5 cloves of garlic grated.
  5. It's 3 teaspoons of curry powder.
  6. It's 1 teaspoon of salt.
  7. It's 1 of vegetable stock cube.
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon of butter.
  9. It's 2 of cloves.
  10. You need Half of a tin of tomatoes.

Instant pot mung beans step by step
