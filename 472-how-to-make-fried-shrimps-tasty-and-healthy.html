Ingredients of Fried Shrimps

  1. You need of Shrimps (500g).
  2. It's 1/2 cup of flour.
  3. It's 2 of Eggs.
  4. Prepare of Bread crumbs.
  5. You need of Salt and Pepper.
  6. Prepare of Vegetable oil for frying.
  7. It's of Shrimp Marinade (optional you could use your own marinade).
  8. You need 1 tbsp of Mustard.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of Lemon juice.
  10. Prepare of Salt and Pepper.
  11. Prepare of Onion and garlic powder.

Fried Shrimps instructions

  1. Marinate the shrimps first for 1 hour in the fridge or preferably overnight..
  2. Crack the nerves of the shrimps to make it flat, so that it doesn't curl up when frying..
  3. Dip the shrimps in the flour first, then the eggs and finally with the bread crumbs. It is better of course to season every station to enhance the flavor..
  4. When done fry in hot oil until the skin gets golden brown and it has reached the preferred color. Check one and if it's not done from the inside just pop them in the oven for 10-15 minutes at 180 degrees C..
  5. Then serve hot on a plate and bon appetit..
