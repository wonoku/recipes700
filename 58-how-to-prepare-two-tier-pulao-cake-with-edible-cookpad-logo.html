Two Tier Pulao Cake With Edible Cookpad Logo instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Drain excess water and set aside..
  2. Now heat ghee in a kadai,add whole spices and mix well..
  3. Add in water and rice and stir.Now add fried raisins,salt and sugar and mix well..
  4. Bring it to a boil.Boil it for 7-8 minutes on high flame till most of the water is absorbed..
  5. Now cover the kadai with a lid and put it on a low heat and cook for another 5-6 minutes..
  6. In the meantime,take saffron in a bowl,add warm milk and stir well.Let it sit for 3 minutes..
  7. Now open the lid and take out half amount of pulao from the kadai with the help of a spatula in a bowl.Set aside for 5 minutes..
  8. Now add saffron milk in the leftover pulao and cook it for another 5 minutes..
  9. Now remove the kadai from heat and switch off the flame.Set aside for 5 minutes..
  10. Now fluff up the yellow coloured pulao in a plate and shape it like a cake.The first layer is ready now..
  11. Now fluff up the white coloured pulao in a different plate and shape it like a cake.Place this yellow coloured pulao cake on the top of the white coloured pulao cake.In this way second layer is done..
  12. Your two tier pulao cake is ready now..
  13. Now pour tomato chutney in a icing bag and draw a Cookpad Logo on the top of the two tier pulao cake..
  14. Garnish the two tier pulao cake with green peas..
  15. Your Two Tier Pulao Cake is ready to serve now..
