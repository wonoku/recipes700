Ingredients of Chocolate chip cookies

  1. Prepare 1 cup of unsalted butter (16 Tbsp), softened.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of white sugar.
  3. You need 1 cup of brown sugar (packed).
  4. Prepare 2 of large eggs (room temperature).
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  6. Prepare 3 cups of all-purpose flour (sifted).
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  8. It's 2 cups of chocolate chips (350 grams).
