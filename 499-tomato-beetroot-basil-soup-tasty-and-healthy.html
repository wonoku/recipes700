Ingredients of Tomato Beetroot Basil soup

  1. It's 5-6 of tomatoes.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of chopped onion.
  3. Prepare 4-5 of garlic flakes.
  4. It's 1/4 inch of ginger.
  5. You need 1-2 of beetroot.

  7. You need of Pudina for serving.
  8. It's 1 Cup of Boiled corn kernels.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of butter or Olive oil.
  10. It's to taste of Salt.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of sugar.
  12. It's 1 tsp of black pepper powder.

Tomato Beetroot Basil soup step by step

  1. Heat butter in a pressure cooker. Put onion chopped slices, ginger and garlic. Saute it untill turns light brown or translucent..
  2. Now add tomatoes, beetroot, basil leaves,black pepper, water, sugar and salt to taste. Allow 2-3 whistles and then switch off the flame..
  3. Now open the pressure cooker and grind the mixture into fine puree. Strain this puree in a sauce pan..
  4. Put boiled corn kernels and finely chopped pudina leaves to enhance the flavour of soup.we can also add black pepper for health benefit..
