Ingredients of Mushroom Risotto Instant Pot IP

  1. Prepare 1/4 (4 Tbs.) of Butter.
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup of oil.
  3. You need 1 of medium onion.
  4. You need 2 cloves of garlic.
  5. You need 12 Oz. of Mushrooms.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp. of Dried Rosemary.
  7. You need 3/4 cup of white wine.
  8. You need 1 quart (4 cups) of chicken broth.
  9. It's 1 1/2 cups of arborio rice.
  10. Prepare 1 of salt to taste.
  11. It's 1 of pepper to taste.
  12. Prepare 3/4 cup of frozen peas (optional).

Mushroom Risotto Instant Pot IP instructions

  1. Chop onion and garlic, slice mushrooms.
  2. Set IP to saute, add butter and oil, Olive oil is the norm, but I didn't like the taste of olive oil, so I am using this one.
  3. When the oil/butter is hot, add mushrooms. Stir until the mushrooms just start to soften, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add onions/garlic. Stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Type of rice to use in this dish, add rice to pot, do not soak rice first..
  6. Stir rice in, about 3 minutes to heat each grain and get it covered in oil. DO NOT SKIP THIS STEP!.
  7. I bought a white wine for cooking, not drinking. I am using this, $4.00 a bottle, worked great in this dish..
  8. Add wine, deglaze, scrap any stuck bits up.
  9. Ready your homemade broth ðŸ˜†.
  10. And add the broth to your pot.
  11. Stir it all up, scrap the sides, get everything down in the liquid..
  12. Add pepper (to taste), Rosemary, salt (taste it!), Stir in..
  13. Place lid on your IP, set your IP to pressure cook, for 6 minutes. Let it do it's thing.
  14. When time is up, QR (quick release) the pressure. After the pin drops, remove lid, turn off IP (no warming). Still looks like a lot of liquid, no fear!.
  15. Optional Peas, Both the wife and I like peas. We like the taste, and more so the little texture difference they add. I'm using frozen..
  16. Optional Peas, add your frozen peas as soon as you open the pot. Stir in..
  17. Stir your risotto until it gets creamy, looking better now..
  18. Grate your parmesan ðŸ˜Š.
  19. Add the cheese to your pot..
  20. Now just stir until the cheese melts, and it's ready!.
  21. You can garnish with some fresh parsley, which I did not have. Place in serving dish for a side, or main course, however you want.
