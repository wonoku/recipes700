Baked Ravioli (Instant Pot) instructions

  1. I use my Instant Pot’s Slow Cook feature for this recipe but you can easily adapt the instructions to a regular slow cooker..
  2. Pour meat sauce into Instant Pot..
  3. Press SAUTÉ—Normal.
  4. Heat for 10 minutes stirring frequently..
  5. Press CANCEL (Keep Warm: On).
  6. Stir in ravioli..
  7. Close & lock the lid..
  8. Press the pressure release handle down to open the vent..
  9. Press SLOW COOK—More; Time: 20 minutes..
  10. Press CANCEL (Keep Warm: Off).
  11. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  12. Transfer ravioli and sauce to a clear glass baking dish..
  13. Create a thick layer of cheese across the top of the dish..
  14. Place uncovered dish in oven for 8 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden..
  15. Serve hot..
