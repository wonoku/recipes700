Fried stuffed Zucchini instructions

  1. Start by cooking your beef, in a pot add 1 tbsp butter, 2 medium sized minced onions, and mastic, wait till caramelized then add the beef+ 2 tsp salt and pepper.
  2. Start with cutting away the heads of zucchini and stripping it's skin (don't completely skin it).
  3. After stripping carefully hollow the zucchini and take care not to pierce it!.
  4. In a large pan add 1 tbsp minced garlic to 2 tbsp butter and mastic then fry the zucchini.
  5. While the zucchini fries and the beef cooks, prepare the sauce (all the taste comes here, enrich it with herbs and flavours!).
  6. Add 2 medium sized minced onions in 1 tbsp butter, mastic, Cardamom and caramelize, then add 4 tomatoes' juice and 3 leaves bay laurel + 2 tbsp heavy cream + salt, pepper and chilli.
  7. Extra tip when adding the Cardamom squeeze it till you get the seeds, use the small seeds in the sauce and put the rest of cardamom with zucchini while frying!.
  8. Afted frying the zucchini on all sides let cool for a while then start stuffing your beef!.
  9. Pour the thickened sauce over the zucchini and add extra beef on top (you can add some mozzarella for a golden tasty crust!).
  10. Put in oven for 10 mins then serve!.
