Ingredients of Chocolate chip banana loaf bread (moist)

  1. Prepare 180 g of All-purpose flour.
  2. You need 100 g of sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 3 of mashed bananas.
  6. You need 1 of egg (lightly beaten).
  7. Prepare 75 g of butter (melted).
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  9. Prepare 100 gr of chocolate chips.
