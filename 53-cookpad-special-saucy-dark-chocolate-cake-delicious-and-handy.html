Cookpad special saucy dark chocolate cake instructions

  1. Take two eggs and add pinch salt in it and beat well. If you beat eggs with salt than it makes best foam. Beat for five minutes..
  2. Add castor sugar slowly slowly and beat again. If you add all castor sugar together so it's flatten the foam of eggs. Beat for five minutes..
  3. Add oil and vanilla essence and beat again for five minutes. This is the last stage of beating with electric beater..
  4. Sieved all purpose flour, coco powder and baking powder three times. The more you sieve the more air passes and the more it gives perfect result. And greased 7inch cake pan also..
  5. Now add sieved flour in egg batter slowly slowly and mix with spatula, never add all flour together, add flour than add milk than flour than milk in this manner mix all together very well..
  6. Pour batter in greased pan and tap on counter. Now bake at 180 degrees for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes insert toothpik in it if it comes clean thats mean cake is ready. Cold your cake for 2 to 3 hours than apply sauce and decoration..
  7. For sauce melt dark chocolate directly on stove by continuous stirring for 2 to 3 minutes. Than add milk and mix instantly..
  8. Apply sauce on cake's top. As it's saucy cake so it never gives smooth surface. Decorate with choco balls and bunties..
